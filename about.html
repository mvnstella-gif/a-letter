About This Page

Why this exists

This page exists for one person only. It isn’t a blog. It isn’t documentation. And it was never meant to be understood by anyone else. It’s a private space where I place things I don’t need to explain, justify, or translate. Thoughts that are too specific to be shared, too honest to be edited, and too quiet to survive loud rooms.

Nothing here asks for interpretation. Nothing here is trying to perform.

If you’re reading this, it’s not because you stumbled onto it. It’s because this page was written with you in mind, long before it had a place to live.

For you, F.
