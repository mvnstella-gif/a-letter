This page is for every time we are one day away from closing that space again, whether it is the first meeting or the fifth.

Hey, love.

You were direct, asking where this could lead, asking about free will like it was the most natural thing to discuss with a stranger you had just met.

Night after night, without really planning it, we just kept showing up for each other. We didn't skip. We didn't fade. It felt steady in a way that surprised me.

And now here we are, one day before everything becomes real. No more screens, no more guessing the shape of your presence, no more imagining how you sound when you're close.

If your heart feels a little fast right now, mine does too. Tomorrow is the moment all those calls, all those nights, all those conversations have been quietly building toward. Breathe, rest, and let yourself feel everything.

So if you are reading this one day before we meet, breathe. Rest. Let the excitement sit with you. Tomorrow is ours, and we will finally see what distance has been preparing us for all this time. See you tomorrow, my love.
