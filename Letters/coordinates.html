CHAPTER 4: Distance

There are moments when I wish proximity could carry what words sometimes fail to hold. And there are moments when distance forces a clarity that closeness might have blurred We cannot rely on habit, we have to rely on presence even when it is mediated by screens. Distance does not weaken what we build, It reveals what survives without convenience :]
