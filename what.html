What We Are Build

The beginning, and everything unfolding after.

I don't imagine us as a fairytales story that resolves itself, but I imagine us as something built slowly with awareness of what can and cannot be rushed.

What we're building isn't dependent on constant reassurance or dramatic moments, but I think it's built through planning, honesty, and the willing to name difficulties instead of avoiding them..

So.. we are factoring in time, health, distance, and also growth in here. Not pretending they don't exist, but asking how we move with them rather than against them.

This isn't about control or certainty, but it's about alignment. About choosing to face reality together instead of constructing illusions separately.

If this continues, I hope this will be sustain. I don't expect it to be perfect either, and I don't expect this to be easy. At least just real enough to last.
